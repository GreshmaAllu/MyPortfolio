About Me

Hi! I’m Allu Greshma, a passionate UI/UX Designer and AI-powered Web/App Developer from India. I’m currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science at Centurion University.

My design journey began with curiosity for how interfaces shape user emotions and evolved into crafting purposeful digital products that truly connect with people. I enjoy creating visually appealing, intuitive, and data-driven designs that merge creativity with technology.

I specialize in using tools like Figma, Canva, and Android Studio (Kotlin) to transform ideas into seamless interactive experiences.
